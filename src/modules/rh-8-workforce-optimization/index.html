The Cost of Rural Staffing

Critical Access Hospitals often spend 30-50% more per hour for **Locum Tenens** providers due to recruitment scarcity. However, the model show that investing in **Generalist Training** (e.g., ER physicians who can handle basic inpatient rounding) creates a "buffer" that stabilizes quality when permanent staff leave.
