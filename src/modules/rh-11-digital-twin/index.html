Discrete Event Simulation (DES)

In a small-N hospital, **resource conflict** is the primary driver of delay. A single x-ray machine serves the ER, Inpatients, and Outpatients. Using DES, we model the facility not as an average throughput, but as a sequence of discrete events where patients compete for **Shared Constrained Resources**.
