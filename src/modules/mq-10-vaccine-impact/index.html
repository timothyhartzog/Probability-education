The SIRV Model

This simulator uses a modified SIRV (Susceptible-Infected-Recovered-Vaccinated) compartmental model to track the flow of a population through different health states. When a portion of the population is vaccinated, the effective reproduction number $R_e$ is reduced.

Herd Immunity Threshold: For a disease with basic reproduction number $R_0$, the minimum proportion of the population that must be immune (through infection or vaccination) to stop the spread is: $$H_t = 1 - \frac{1}{R_0}$$
