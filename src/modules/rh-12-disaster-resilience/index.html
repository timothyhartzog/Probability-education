The Triage-to-Transfer Dilemma

In an urban disaster, the primary bottleneck is **Triage Speed**. In a rural disaster, the primary bottleneck is **Transfer Throughput** (ambulances and aircraft). We model local resilience using the **START Triage Algorithm** and a limited-capacity egress queue, highlighting the "Golden Hour" threshold for survivors.
