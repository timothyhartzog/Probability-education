Connectivity and R₀ in Rural Areas

Unlike urban centers with continuous exposure, rural disease spread is often characterized by **Stochastic Jump Processes**. Long periods of zero infection are interrupted by **Super-Spreader Events** (community fairs, church gatherings, or regional schools). The lack of daily background exposure can lead to rapid "Flash Outbreaks" that overwhelm CAHs.
