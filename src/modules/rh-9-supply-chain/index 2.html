The Zero-Inventory Problem

Critical Access Hospitals (CAHs) cannot economically store rare blood types or expensive biologics. This creates a **Zero-Inventory Trap** where a single trauma patient can deplete the regional stock. **Drone networks** (Zipline-style) transform this linear logistical problem into a spatial network problem, effectively "expanding" the pharmacy radius of a hub hospital.
