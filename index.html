Textbook References

  • Billingsley, P. Probability and Measure (3rd ed.). Wiley. Measure theory foundations, convergence theorems, characteristic functions.
  • Durrett, R. Probability: Theory and Examples (5th ed.). Cambridge. Laws of large numbers, CLT, martingales, Markov chains, ergodic theory.
  • Feller, W. An Introduction to Probability Theory and Its Applications, Vol. I & II. Wiley. Discrete probability, generating functions, renewal theory, Brownian motion.
  • Grimmett, G. & Stirzaker, D. Probability and Random Processes (4th ed.). Oxford. Random processes, branching processes, queuing theory, diffusions.
  • Shiryaev, A. N. Probability (2nd ed.). Springer. Rigorous measure-theoretic treatment, optional stopping, stochastic integration.
  • Kallenberg, O. Foundations of Modern Probability (3rd ed.). Springer. Deep measure theory, disintegration, Palm measures, Levy processes.
